# Customer-Segmentation-Behavioral-Profiling-Dashboard-Power-BI-

##  Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer behavior and segmenting customers into meaningful tiers using Power BI and DAX. The goal is to identify high-value customers, understand engagement patterns, and uncover opportunities to improve customer retention and bonus utilization.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer activity based on loyalty, bonus usage behavior, and overall performance scores.

---

##  Objectives

* Segment customers into tiers based on behavioral and performance metrics
* Analyze customer engagement using loyalty and bonus usage scores
* Identify high-value and low-value customer groups
* Evaluate bonus utilization efficiency across different customer segments
* Provide actionable insights for customer retention and value optimization

---

##  Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer-level and monthly behavioral data with the following features:

* **Primary City** – Customer location
* **Account Number** – Unique customer identifier
* **Customer Type** – Category of customer
* **Loyalty Score** – Measure of customer engagement and consistency
* **Bonus Usage Score** – Measure of how effectively customers use allocated bonuses
* **Month Bonus Used** – Number of months bonus was utilized
* **Month Bonus Not Used** – Number of months bonus was not utilized
* **Final Score** – Combined performance score
* **Score %** – Normalized score representation
* **Customer Tier** – Segmentation label (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)

---

##  Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Data transformation and metric calculations
* **Excel** – Initial data handling and preparation

---

##  Key Metrics & Calculations

* Average Customer Usage
* Bonus Usage Rate
* Loyalty Score Analysis
* Customer Tier Distribution
* Score Percentage Distribution
* Months Bonus Used vs Not Used (Behavioral Analysis)

---

##  Dashboard Features

* KPI cards highlighting total customers, average score, and tier distribution
* Customer tier breakdown for segmentation insights
* Behavioral analysis of bonus usage patterns
* Customer type performance comparison
* Geographic distribution of customers by city
* Identification of top high-value customers

---

##  Key Insights

* A large proportion of customers fall into lower tiers, indicating potential for value improvement
* High-tier customers demonstrate stronger loyalty and better bonus utilization
* Some customer segments receive bonuses but do not utilize them effectively
* Customer value varies significantly across different customer types and locations

---

##  Business Recommendations

* Implement targeted campaigns to convert mid-tier customers into high-value customers
* Optimize bonus allocation strategies to improve utilization rates
* Focus retention efforts on high-performing customer segments
* Develop engagement strategies for low-activity customers

---

##  Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data-driven segmentation can provide actionable insights into customer behavior. By leveraging Power BI and DAX, businesses can better understand their customers and make informed decisions to improve engagement and profitability.
